Tattoo Salvation was opened 2012 by veteran tattooer Adam Potts to offer world class tattooing in the most professional and cleanest setting possible. The shop is located in the heart of Louisville's Highlands neighborhood at 1412 Bardstown Rd. As the owner and sole tattooer at Tattoo Salvation Adam is well versed in all styles of tattooing, although, he specializes in the traditional Americana style.

Tattoos are done by appointment OR as walk-ins. Come in for a consultation, or schedule your appointment online!
